Opportunity Index

King County, WA

This map is assessing the educational opportunity index in King County Washington. An opportunity index is an evaluation of multiple factors that contribute to the educational opportunity in King County. This assessment looked at five vairables, each are listed below. Each variable was given a weight that contributes to the statistical analysis performed in the development of the opportunity index.
Variables

  • Reading Test Scores; Weight 10%
  • Math Test Scores; Weight 10%
  • Teacher Qualifications; Weight 20%
  • Student Poverty; Weight 30%
  • Graduation Rate; Weight 30%
