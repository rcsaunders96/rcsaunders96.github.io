Marquesas Keys predicted landcover change due sea level rise in 2100

These images display landcover change for the Marquesas Keys in the year 2100 for sea level rise from 1ft to 4ft. The first image in the upper left corner is the initial condition of the Marquesas Keys in the year 2000. These images were built using SLAMM and ArcGIS Maps. This work was completed Fall of 2017 in collaboration with Dr. Jason Evans, Associate Professor of Environmental Science and Studies as Stetson University.
