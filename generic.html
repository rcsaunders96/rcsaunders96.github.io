Education

Education is the most powerful weapon
which you can use to change the world.
- Nelson Mandela

Rachel obtained her BS in Environmental Science & Geography from Stetson University May 2018, with Minors in Political Science and Environmental Studies. She is also a proud alumna of the Alpha Xi Delta Omega chapter where she served on the executive board for two years; developing leadership skills and helping women realize their potential. At the end of her sophomore year Rachel accepted a marketing position with the Center for Community Engagement, CCE, on campus. She held this position until she graduated. You can find out more about this her work with the CCE in the Experience tab. Her greatest accomplishment while at Stetson was her senior research, which focused on water quality testing at Stetson University. Go to the Portfolio tab to learn more about her research, and to find other samples of her work.

Rachel is currently working on her MS in Geospatial Technologies from the University of Washington Tacoma. Her anticipated graduation date is August 2019. She is the Teaching Assistant for the 311 GIS introductory course for 2018-2019. Over the course of next year, she will produce a capstone project. For more information on the MS Geospatial Technologies degree please click here.
